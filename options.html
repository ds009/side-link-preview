Side Link Preview

Changes are saved automatically. Reload the page for them to take effect.

Language

Language of the settings page and side panel UI.

Scope

One domain per line. Subdomains are matched automatically — entering example.com also matches www.example.com and blog.example.com. The * wildcard is also supported (e.g. *keyword* matches any domain containing keyword).

Trigger

Click: open the link in the side panel on click. Hover: automatic preview after hovering longer than the delay.

Hover delay ms
Hover preview requires the side panel to already be open. The first time, click the toolbar side-panel icon or the extension icon to open it.
Want a link to skip the Side Panel? Hold ⌘ / Ctrl / Shift / Alt while clicking — it will open in a new tab as usual. To always skip a whole site, add its domain under Scope above.

Link scope

By default only target="_blank" links (the ones a page asks to open in a new tab) are intercepted. Enable the option below to also send same-tab (non-_blank) links to the side panel.
