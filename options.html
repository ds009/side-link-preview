Language

Language of the settings page and side panel UI.

Scope

One domain per line. Subdomains are matched automatically — entering example.com also matches www.example.com and blog.example.com. The * wildcard is also supported (e.g. *keyword* matches any domain containing keyword).

Link scope

By default only target="_blank" links (the ones a page asks to open in a new tab) are intercepted. Enable the option below to also send same-tab (non-_blank) links to the side panel.

Want a link to skip the Side Panel? Hold ⌘ / Ctrl for a new tab, ⇧ for a new window, or ⌥ Option / Alt to open in the current tab. To always skip a whole site, add its domain under Scope above.
